Virtual Lab

As my senior year project, I worked on a team to help further the development of a virtual lab that would allow student to gain experience performing lab activities regardless of their location or financial status. We were sponsored by the CU Biofrontiers program and were in charge of adding another lab module to the already existing game, as well as updating all of the code to be compatible with Godot 4.0.

Featured Skills: Godot Game engine, 2D sprite creation
